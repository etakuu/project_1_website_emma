Japans major city

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan. The greater Tokyo Area is one of the most populous metropolitan area in the world. There are a total of 23 special wards in Tokyo. They occupy what is now known as Toyko Metropolis. Before 1943 it was known as Tokyo City.The special wards were made by the Japanese Local Autonomy Act and is unique to Tokyo.Tokyo Metropolis is one of the 47 prefectures of Japan. As of July 31st, 2016 there are 13.62 million people in Tokyo. In 2013 there were only 3.884 million in Los Angeles.

Harajuku

Harajuku district in Shibuya, Tokyo is the epicenter of street fashion. In the 1990s to the 2000s the rise of steet faashion in Harajuku was spectacular. In Harajuku almost every person you spot is dressed crazy. Women wear crazy colored wigs and big colorful platform boots with neon tights and a huge pastel poofy dress on. But others will have black hair and a mountain of white face paint and a whole palette of black eyeshadow on. They might also wear black corsets and big black boots. They adopted this dark style from Hirari Ikeda.
